My Bio

Experiencing at the moment is hard, but when it becomes memory, it feels like heaven.

This is the true picture of my college life. With outstanding performance in high school, I entered Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics without taking Chinese College Entrance Examination. Back then I was confident and hopeful and firmly believed I was much cooler than Tom Cruise in TopGun, because I was going to design airplanes!

However, in reality, college is a place of eagle boiling. It discovers a true you and it tests your loyalty towards dream. Facing constantly exposed shortcomings and unexpected reality, I grow mature and wise in suffering.
